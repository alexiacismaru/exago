Exago? What is that?

Exago is a strategic tiling game that will be instantly familiar to anyone who has played Connect Four or tic-tac-toe.
 
Exago succeeds not in that its game play mechanics are especially original but in that it is so well-designed, not to mention colorful, concise, and fun for the players. Each new tile must be placed adjacent to another one (not necessarily of their own color).
 
The first to have four pieces in a straight line wins the game.
